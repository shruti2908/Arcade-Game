The Arcade Game

Use your arrow keys to reach to the other side in 60 seconds and gain a point!

Grab as many of these as you can!

  • ★ The Blues - They 'll slow you by a second!
  • ★ The Greens- 2 pts
  • ★ The Orangies - 5 pts
  • ★ Hearts - One life

Avoid following items :(

  • ❌ Bug - lose one life!
  • ❌ Water - lose one life!
  • ❌ Rock - lose one life!
